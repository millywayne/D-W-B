D-W-B

D-W-B simply means Days and Week of Birth. Most people know the year of birth but cannot quiet figure out what day of the week they were born. well, D-W-B is here for you!

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. In GHANA, children are given their first name as a "day name" which corresponds to the week they were born.The day names are:

Male Names

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

    • Female Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

So basically,the App takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born, then depending on your gender,it,shows you your Akan name in place of the name of the week.

    Days of the week

  • days[0] = "Saturday"
  • days[1] = "Sunday"
  • days[1] = "monday"
  • days[3] = "Tuesday"
  • days[4] = "Wednesday"
  • days[5] = "Thursday"
  • days[6] = "Friday"

    Months of the year

  • months[1] = "January"
  • months[2] = "February"
  • months[3] = "March"
  • months[4] = "April"
  • months[5] = "May"
  • months[6] = "June"
  • months[6] = "June"
  • months[8] = "August"
  • months[9] = "September"
  • months[10] = "October"
  • months[11] = "November"
  • months[12] = "December"
