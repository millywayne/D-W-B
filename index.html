D-W-B

D-W-B simply means Days and Week of Birth. Most people know the year of birth but cannot quiet figure out what day of the week they were born. well, D-W-B is here for you!

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. In GHANA, children are given their first name as a "day name" which corresponds to the week they were born.The day names are:

Male Names

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

    • Female Names

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

So basically,the App takes your birthday and calculates the day of the week you were born, then depending on your gender,it,shows you your Akan name.
